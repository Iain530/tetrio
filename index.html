TETRIO

Player 1 Controls

W - Rotate
A - Move left
S - Increase speed
D - Move right

Goal

Help your friend escape the falling blocks by building a path out.

You must achieve the score aim before your friend can escape.

Be careful though if you crush your friend you lose!

100 points per row cleared.

Goal

You have been trapped in a world of falling blocks. Your only chance to escape is with the help of your friend. The only way out is up, but watch out for falling blocks!

You must achieve the score aim to unlock the door before you can proceed.

50 points per coin collected.

Player 2 Controls

LEFT  - Move left
RIGHT - Move right
UP    - Jump
